The Original Recipe

Here's what was described in the video (more or less).

  1. Grab your big ole kitchenaid stand mixer and throw in that butter and sugar to start mixing!

  2. Gradually increase the speed and scraping the sides every 3 minutes and slowly add the 2 eggs, one at a time, whenever it looks halfway done throught the creaming process (around 15 mins in from creaming).

  3. In a seperate bowl, add all of the dry ingredients together and wisk evenly to distribute the salt and baking soda in the flour.

  4. After the the creaming process has dissoveled all of the sugar, and the consistancy is silky like, add in the dry ingredients using a siff to remove any clumps of leavening.

  5. Add chocolate chips, but don't mix for too long or you'll develop gluten in the flour and make a tough cookie.

  6. Lastly, scoop them out onto a baking tray with parchment paper, and place them in the oven (preheated to 350 degrees fahrenheit) for 8-12 minutes depending on size.
