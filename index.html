Varying Ingredients:

This is for a Thin and Crips cookie.

  • 2 and a 1/2 stick of unsalted butter, softend.
  • 1 and 1/4 cups of granulated sugar.
  • 3/4 cup of brown sugar (Dark prefered).

This is for a Soft and Chewy cookie.

  • 2 sticks of unsalted butter, softend.
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar.
  • 1 cup of brown sugar (Drak prefered).

This is for a Light and Cakey cookie.

  • 1 and 3/4 sticks of unsalted butter, softend.
  • 3/4 cup of granulated sugar.
  • 1/4 cup of brown sugar (Dark prefered).

Constant Ingredients:

Dry Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon of uniodized salt.
  • Half (1/2) a teaspoon of baking soda.
  • 2 and 1/4 cup of flour.

Wet Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoon of Vanilla Extract.
  • 2 Eggs.
